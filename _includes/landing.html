Why the Shack?

The Shack is all about free life, rediscovering the joy of hacking away without a care in the world. Remember the kicks we had with silly contraptions as kids? Tinker Shack celebrates freedom and craftsmanship! What have you been up to lately?

Fluffy

Fluffy watches and faithfully reports Linux on-disk filesystem events. While Fluffy is a convenient CLI tool, libfluffy performs all the magic under the hood with the help of inotify kernel subsystem.
